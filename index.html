About Me

I am Saeed Hossen Rakib, a dedicated Physical Design Engineer with 2 years and 8 months of experience specializing in RTL to GDSII design. My expertise spans across synthesis, physical verification, PnR, STA, and more, with hands-on work up to 5nm technology.
